Privacy

Blue Label Lab is published as a static informational site. It does not ask readers to create an account, log in, upload files, enter payment details, or submit private workplace information while reading the pages.

The hosting provider or content delivery network may still create normal technical logs. Those records can include requested file, timestamp, IP-derived region, user agent, referrer, status code, or similar delivery information used for security and troubleshooting.

Outbound links may lead to LeStallion, marketplaces, manufacturers, image-credit pages, documentation, or other third-party sites. Once you follow an external link, that destination controls its own cookies, analytics, affiliate tracking, checkout flows, and privacy policy.

Because this site has no reader account system, it is not designed to store a profile of your research. Avoid sending confidential business details through any related contact path unless a separate destination specifically requires them and you trust that destination.

If the site later adds forms, comments, analytics dashboards, newsletters, or account features, this privacy note should be updated before those features go live. Until then, treat it as a static reading layer with ordinary hosting logs and external-link boundaries.
