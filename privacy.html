Privacy

This privacy page explains the limited data posture of Blue Label Lab. The resource is published as static HTML, CSS, and image files for general product-research reading.

The site does not include user accounts, comments, checkout, lead forms, newsletter signup, product registration, file uploads, embedded support forms, or private messaging.

The pages are not designed to collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, payment details, order numbers, product serial numbers, workplace policies, device contents, employer information, or work schedules.

Routine hosting systems may process basic technical information such as IP address, browser type, requested path, timestamp, and referring URL for delivery, caching, security, and abuse prevention.

External links may lead to retailers, manufacturers, manuals, or reference sites with their own privacy practices. Review those policies before sharing personal or business information.

Privacy is separate from About and Warehouse. About explains editorial purpose, while Warehouse explains safe correction boundaries.

Use the site for general planning, verify product details at the source, and keep sensitive workplace or equipment information in private channels rather than public web pages.
